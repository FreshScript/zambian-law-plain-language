Marriage Law in Zambia

Marriage in Zambia can be either under customary law or statutory (civil) law.

1. Customary Marriage

  • Based on traditional customs and approved by both families.
  • Can involve lobola (bride price) and traditional ceremonies.
  • It is legally recognized if all customs are followed correctly.

2. Statutory Marriage

  • Officially registered at a civic office or church licensed to marry.
  • Only allows one spouse at a time (monogamous).
  • Must be aged 21+ or have parental consent if younger.

3. Key Legal Facts

  • You cannot be married under both systems at once.
  • Divorce is handled differently depending on the marriage type.
  • Marriages must be between consenting adults.
